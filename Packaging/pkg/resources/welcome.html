Welcome to Paper Overlay

Paper Overlay puts a subtle, paper-like grain over your screen to make long hours in front of your Mac easier on the eyes. It lives in your menu bar, works across all your monitors, and never gets in the way — clicks go straight through it.

This installer will place Paper Overlay in your Applications folder and start it for you. Click Continue to proceed.

About the security warning you just clicked through: Paper Overlay is an independent app that isn't notarized through Apple's developer program, so macOS shows a caution dialog the first time you open the installer. That "Open Anyway" step in System Settings was a one-time thing — the installed app itself will open normally, with no further warnings, and it never asks for any system permissions.
