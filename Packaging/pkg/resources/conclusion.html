Paper Overlay is installed 🎉

Paper Overlay lives in your menu bar — look for the small page icon near the clock, at the top-right of your screen. If it isn't there yet, open Paper Overlay from your Applications folder.

  • Click the icon to open the dashboard: pick a preset or fine-tune the color, opacity, and grain.
  • Want it always on? Enable Start at Login at the bottom of the dashboard.

You won't see any more security warnings — that step is behind you. Enjoy easier reading!
