PAPER OVERLAY — IF THE APP WON'T OPEN

macOS blocks apps from independent developers that aren't notarized
through Apple's developer program. You only have to get past this once:

  1. Drag PaperOverlay into the Applications folder.
  2. Open it. macOS will say it "Not Opened" — click "Done"
     (NOT "Move to Bin").
  3. Open System Settings -> Privacy & Security.
  4. Scroll down: you'll see "PaperOverlay was blocked...".
     Click "Open Anyway" and confirm.

After that, Paper Overlay opens normally forever. It runs in your menu
bar — look for the small page icon near the clock.

Tip: the .pkg installer version of Paper Overlay (available from the same
download page) walks you through installation and avoids this dance for
the app itself.
